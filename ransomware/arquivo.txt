
Letras de metal e rock frequentemente apresentam uma variedade de temas, incluindo filosofia, reflexões sobre a vida e a sociedade. Aqui estão algumas frases filosóficas tiradas de letras desses gêneros:

Metal:

"So close, no matter how far, couldn't be much more from the heart. Forever trusting who we are, and nothing else matters." - Metallica, "Nothing Else Matters"

"The only way to do great work is to love what you do." - Opeth, "The Grand Conjuration"

"We are the ones with the power to hold every living soul." - Sabaton, "Primo Victoria"

"In a world so far away, at the end of a closing day. A little child was born and raised, deep in the forest on a hidden place." - Nightwish, "Elvenpath"

Rock:

"I wish you would step back from that ledge, my friend. You could cut ties with all the lies that you've been living in." - Third Eye Blind, "Jumper"

"I get knocked down, but I get up again. You're never gonna keep me down!" - Chumbawamba, "Tubthumping"

"Don't stop believin', hold on to that feeling." - Journey, "Don't Stop Believin'"

"You say you want a revolution, well, you know, we all want to change the world." - The Beatles, "Revolution"
